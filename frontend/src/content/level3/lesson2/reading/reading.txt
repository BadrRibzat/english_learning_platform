
People use different types of transportation to travel from one place to another. Cars are very common and can travel fast on roads. Buses carry many people at once and are used for public transport. Bicycles are powered by pedaling and are good for short distances. Trains run on tracks and can carry many passengers or goods. Airplanes fly in the sky and are the fastest way to travel long distances. Boats are used to travel on water. Motorcycles are fast and can weave through traffic easily.
