Read the following passage and answer the questions below:

Title: Shapes Around Us

Look around your room and identify shapes and colors you see. Describe them using the vocabulary learned in this lesson.

Questions:
1. How many shapes can you identify in your room?
2. What colors are the shapes you identified?
3. Can you describe one shape using "this is" and another using "these are"?
