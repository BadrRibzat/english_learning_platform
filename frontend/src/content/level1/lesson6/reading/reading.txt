Read the following sentences and answer the questions below:

---
1. The dog barked loudly.
2. Cats like to sleep in warm places.
3. Birds build nests in trees.
4. Elephants have long tusks.
5. Fish swim in water.
6. Turtles have shells to protect them.
7. Lions are known as the kings of the jungle.
8. Monkeys are playful animals.

Questions:
1. What did the dog do?
2. Where do cats like to sleep?
3. What do birds build?
4. What do elephants have?
5. Where do fish swim?
6. What do turtles have to protect them?
7. What are lions known as?
8. What are monkeys like?
