Read the following sentences and answer the questions below:

---
1. The sky is blue.
2. She wore a red dress to the party.
3. The sunflowers are yellow.
4. The grass in the park is green.
5. Purple is my favorite color.
6. Oranges are orange in color.
7. The baby's room is painted pink.
8. His dog has brown fur.

Questions:
1. What color is the sky?
2. What did she wear to the party?
3. What color are the sunflowers?
4. What color is the grass in the park?
5. What is your favorite color?
6. What color are oranges?
7. What color is the baby's room?
8. What color is his dog's fur?
