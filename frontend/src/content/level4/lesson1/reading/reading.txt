
There are many countries around the world, each with its own culture and language. Canada is known for its natural beauty and hockey. Brazil is famous for its carnival and samba music. Japan is renowned for its technology and sushi. France is celebrated for its art, fashion, and cuisine. Australia is known for its wildlife, such as kangaroos and koalas. India is rich in history, spices, and Bollywood films. Egypt is famous for its ancient pyramids and the Nile River.
